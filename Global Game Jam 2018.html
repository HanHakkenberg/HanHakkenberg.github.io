The Game

The Global Game Jam Game is a still unnamed game made for the Global Game Jam 2018. The game has been mad in 48 hours by 2 developers and 2 hybrids. In the game you play as a bat which thries to get a letter filled with food to his baby bats. To do this he will have to move trough a cave with a multitude of trabs and obstacles. These trabs are invisible and will only be revealed when the player hits them with his sonic sound waves. When the player gets hit he wil lose a life depending on the dificulty the player can have 1,3,5 sets of lives. At the end of the first and only level the player will have to face a boss.

My Contribution

In the game we all colaborated with programming as well as 2D art. This was nessesery because of the limited time frame.
